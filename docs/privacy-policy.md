## Introduction

Welcome to Aerologue ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our flight tracking application and related services (the "Service").

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By using the Service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide

| Data Type | When Collected | Purpose |
|-----------|----------------|---------|
| **Account Information** | Registration | Email address, display name, password |
| **Profile Information** | Optional profile setup | Nationality, preferred airlines, profile photo |
| **Communication Data** | In-app messaging | Messages sent to other users |
| **Support Data** | When you contact us | Support tickets, feedback |

### 1.2 Information Collected Automatically

| Data Type | When Collected | Purpose |
|-----------|----------------|---------|
| **Usage Data** | App usage | Features used, time spent, interactions |
| **Device Information** | App launch | Device type, OS version, app version |
| **Log Data** | Each request | IP address, timestamps, errors |

### 1.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

- Your device's precise location (we use flight data from ADS-B, not your GPS)
- Biometric data
- Health information
- Financial account details (payments processed by Stripe)

## 4. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as necessary to provide the Service and comply with legal obligations.

| Data Type | Retention Period | After Deletion |
|-----------|------------------|----------------|
| Account data | Until you delete | 30-day recovery period |
| Flight history | 2 years | Permanently deleted |
| Messages | 90 days | Permanently deleted |
| Crossing records | 1 year | Anonymized |
| Analytics | Aggregated indefinitely | N/A (not personal) |

When you delete your account:
1. Your data enters a 30-day grace period (in case you change your mind)
2. After 30 days, personal data is permanently deleted
3. Some data may be anonymized for statistical purposes

## 6. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data:

- **Encryption**: All data encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256)
- **Access Control**: Role-based access, principle of least privilege
- **Authentication**: Secure password requirements, optional MFA
- **Monitoring**: 24/7 security monitoring and anomaly detection
- **Auditing**: All data access is logged and reviewed

No method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 13. Additional Information for Specific Regions

### 13.1 European Union (GDPR)

If you are in the EU, you have additional rights under GDPR:
- Right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority
- Right to data portability
- Right to be forgotten

Legal basis for processing:
- **Contract**: Account management, service delivery
- **Consent**: Marketing, optional features
- **Legitimate Interest**: Security, fraud prevention, analytics

### 13.2 California (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:
- Know what personal information we collect
- Request deletion of your information
- Opt out of the sale of personal information (we don't sell data)
- Non-discrimination for exercising your rights

To exercise CCPA rights, email privacy@aerologue.app with subject "CCPA Request."

### 13.3 Brazil (LGPD)

If you are in Brazil, you have rights similar to GDPR including:
- Confirmation of data processing
- Access to your data
- Correction of inaccurate data
- Anonymization or deletion of unnecessary data
- Data portability
